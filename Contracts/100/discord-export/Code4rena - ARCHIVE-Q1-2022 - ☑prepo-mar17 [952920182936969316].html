Hello, what's the point of having both an _allowedAccounts and _blockedAccounts access control in AccountAccessController.sol? (edited)
Xavier | prePO 🔮 19-Mar-22 06:30 AM
by default, no one is allowed. a user can change themselves to allowed if they can show that theyre in the merkle tree of allowed addresses. but any address on the block list will be blocked regardless of whether theyre allowed or not. this means we can individually block without needing to update the whole merkle tree again. feel free to DM me for further questions/clarifications!
